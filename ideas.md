# Ideas

1. **Autonomous drone food delivery network**
A nationwide fleet of delivery drones that replaces gig couriers and cuts last-mile costs to near zero for every restaurant and grocer.

2. **Drone-based neighborhood security**
Always-on autonomous patrol drones that respond to alarms and 911 calls in seconds, becoming the default private security layer for suburbs and HOAs.

3. **AI translator for animal communication**
A foundation model trained on animal vocalizations and body language that lets humans meaningfully communicate with pets, livestock, and wildlife.

4. **AI tax preparer**
An end-to-end agent that files taxes for individuals and businesses by pulling directly from banks, payroll, and the IRS, replacing TurboTax and CPAs.

5. **Centralized memory system for organizations**
A persistent company brain that ingests every doc, message, and meeting so employees and AI agents can instantly recall anything the org has ever known.

6. **AI meeting and time orchestrator**
An AI chief of staff that runs your meetings, enforces agendas, and restructures your calendar in real time around what actually matters.

7. **AI city planner**
A simulation-driven platform that designs zoning, transit, and infrastructure for entire cities, used by every municipal government in the world.

8. **Contractor-to-jobs marketplace**
A global platform that matches skilled trades with residential and commercial jobs, becoming the Uber of construction and home services.

9. **AI negotiation and price-quoting agent**
An AI that calls vendors, insurers, and service providers on your behalf to negotiate bills and quotes, saving households and businesses billions annually.

10. **Personal data marketplace**
A platform where users sell their dashcam footage, location history, and other personal data directly to companies, turning every person into a data business.

11. **Airbnb for screen space**
A marketplace where anyone can rent ad space on any TV or display anywhere — bars, gyms, lobbies, storefronts, dorms — turning every screen on earth into bookable out-of-home inventory.

12. **AI company founder**
An autonomous agent that spins up landing pages, products, and ad campaigns to test startup ideas in parallel, launching thousands of validated companies a year.

13. **Self-optimizing website A/B tester**
An AI that continuously redesigns and tests every element of a website to maximize conversion, becoming the default growth layer for every site on the internet.

14. **AI lobbying and public opinion engine**
An AI that runs full-scale advocacy campaigns to shift public opinion and pass legislation on issues like nuclear energy, replacing traditional lobbying firms.

15. **AI security vulnerability predictor**
An AI that predicts and patches physical and digital security flaws in real-world systems before they're exploited, from power grids to airports.

16. **AI industry report generator**
An AI analyst that produces McKinsey-quality industry reports on demand for any sector, displacing consulting firms and market research incumbents.

17. **AI-designed merch and print-on-demand empire**
An AI that designs stickers, posters, and trinkets, runs the storefronts, and manages inventory and fulfillment end-to-end, becoming the largest consumer goods brand online.

18. **Inventory platform for restaurants and service businesses**
A unified inventory and supply management system for every restaurant, salon, and service business, becoming the Shopify of operations.

19. **AI pricing engine for startups and enterprises**
An AI that sets optimal prices for any product or service using real-time willingness-to-pay data, becoming the default pricing layer for global commerce.

20. **AI customer interview platform**
An AI that conducts thousands of nuanced customer interviews in parallel and synthesizes insights, replacing user research teams at every company.

21. **Deep-insight screen time tracker**
A screen time app with radically detailed behavioral analytics that becomes the definitive attention and productivity layer across all your devices.

22. **AWS for a single robotics niche**
The default perception, planning, and fleet software stack for one specific category of robots, becoming the infrastructure layer every company in that niche runs on.

23. **AI-driven SMB rollup**
An AI that scans the economy to find boring businesses whose back office can be gutted and replaced with agents, then acquires and operates them as a private equity roll-up.

24. **AI universal buyer-seller matchmaker**
An AI that watches every channel, industry, and medium to instantly match buyers with the right service providers, becoming the default discovery and transaction layer for global commerce.

25. **AI brain for building management systems**
A pure-software agent that plugs into every commercial building's existing BMS over BACnet and autonomously runs HVAC, lighting, and energy, becoming the default intelligence layer for every building on earth.

26. **Startup brain with tiered role-based visibility**
A company brain that records every meeting and ingests every document, then exposes it through Discord-style tiered roles so each person — founder, exec, IC, contractor — instantly sees exactly the slice of org knowledge they're cleared for and nothing they aren't.
