# Startup Ideas

## AI-Native Customer Acquisition Service
AI that finds B2B companies their best customers by testing hundreds of distribution channels simultaneously and doubling down on what works.

## AI-Native Property Manager
Swallow the property management company — AI handles tenant comms, maintenance dispatch, rent collection, cleaning coordination, Airbnb management, everything.

## Franchise OS
AI that runs every aspect of a physical business location — anyone can open a laundromat, gym, tutoring center, or med spa, and the system handles operations, hiring, scheduling, inventory, marketing, and compliance; you take a revenue cut from every location forever.

## Autonomous Pricing Engine
Dynamically reprices every product/service for SMBs based on demand, competition, and margins in real time.

## Uber for Everything
Platform that lets anyone become a solo service business (cleaning, handyman, landscaping, tutoring, anything) — AI handles advertising, deal flow, calendar, negotiation, invoicing, and purchasing; you just show up and do the work.

## AI-Native Advertising Company
AI that designs creative, buys media, places billboards/digital/print/signage, optimizes spend, and reports results — a full-service ad agency run by two people instead of two hundred.

## AI Demand & Inventory Brain
AI that predicts demand and manages inventory for any physical business — restaurants, retail, vending, warehouses — auto-orders from optimal suppliers, minimizes waste, and saves money.

## One Video → Every Format
One video recording gets turned into clips, blogs, tweets, newsletters, and ads automatically — a production service, not a tool.

## AI-Native Building Services Company
AI that handles inspection, quoting, contractor dispatch, and project management across all building maintenance — roofing, HVAC, electrical, plumbing, painting — swallowing the entire commercial building services industry.

## Amazon-in-a-Box for Small Business
AI that gives every small business the same superpowers Amazon spent $100B building — dynamic pricing, demand prediction, personalized marketing, supply chain optimization, and customer analytics — delivered as a service.

## Physical World Arbitrage Engine
AI that detects price differences across locations, excess inventory that should be moved, and demand/supply mismatches in the physical economy — then either sells the intelligence or executes the trades.
